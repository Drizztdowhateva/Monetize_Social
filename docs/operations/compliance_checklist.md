# Compliance Checklist

## Disclosures

- [ ] FTC/ASA-style affiliate disclosures present near links
- [ ] Video and social disclosures included where needed
- [ ] Sponsored content labeled clearly

## Brand and Program Rules

- [ ] Trademark bidding policy reviewed
- [ ] Coupon/deal policy reviewed
- [ ] Email marketing restrictions reviewed
- [ ] PPC and direct-linking restrictions reviewed

## Data and Privacy

- [ ] Cookie consent workflow configured
- [ ] Privacy policy reflects analytics/affiliate tracking
- [ ] Data retention policy defined

## Operations

- [ ] 2FA enabled on affiliate/network accounts
- [ ] Shared credentials avoided; use password manager
- [ ] Quarterly compliance review scheduled
